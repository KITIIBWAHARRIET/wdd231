Welcome to Jinja City, Uganda!

Jinja is a vibrant city in eastern Uganda, located on the shores of Lake Victoria and at the source of the River Nile. Known as the “Adventure Capital of East Africa,” it’s famous for white-water rafting, kayaking, and stunning natural scenery. The city also has a rich history, with colonial-era architecture, bustling markets, and thriving agriculture and industry.
